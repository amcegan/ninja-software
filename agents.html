Custom Enterprise AI Agents & Workflows

Reliable AI Agents for Your Organisation – Built with Workflows That You Can Trust

Design and deploy AI agents tailored to your internal teams or customer interactions. We harness structured workflows, incremental rollouts and built‑in evaluation to ensure your agents are predictable, secure and ready for enterprise scale. Our approach follows the principles outlined by Dudekula & Dudekula in Engineering Reliable AI Agents & Workflows, where first‑principles thinking and a workflow‑first mindset drive reliability.

The Challenge

Generic AI chatbots and prompt chains often break down in production because they lack structure and observability. Without orchestration and evaluation, non‑deterministic systems become unpredictable and hard to trust. Organisations need agents that integrate tightly with business workflows, maintain compliance, and deliver reliable results even as data and contexts change.

Our Philosophy

We follow a workflow‑first and crawl‑walk‑run methodology inspired by the LasaAI founders. Identify the right problem, ship small and scale deliberately. Build evaluation and guardrails into every workflow from day one. Augment your team rather than replacing them.

How We Build Reliable Agents

Workflow Orchestration

We design deterministic, multi‑step workflows that constrain the behaviour of your agents. By orchestrating tasks, validations and fallbacks, we ensure each step is predictable and auditable. This mirrors the book's emphasis on orchestration over raw model intelligence.

Incremental Deployment

We launch in phases, proving value early before expanding capabilities. We start with a minimal feature set and gradually add more complex behaviours. This crawl‑walk‑run approach reduces risk and allows us to evaluate and refine.

Built‑In Evaluation

All workflows include metrics, evaluation hooks, and human‑in‑the‑loop checkpoints. You get visibility into agent decisions and the ability to intervene when necessary. Evaluation is not an afterthought but part of the design.

Multi‑Agent Coordination

For complex tasks, we orchestrate multiple specialised agents with shared memory and clear interfaces. This architecture improves reliability, as recommended by experts building production‑grade agentic AI systems.

Service Areas

Internal Agents

We build AI agents for employee‑facing workflows: knowledge assistants, compliance advisors, IT support bots, process automation, and more. By integrating with internal systems and enforcing human review, we ensure your workforce is empowered—not automated away.

Customer‑Facing Agents

Design chatbots and service agents that provide accurate answers, process requests, and hand off to humans when needed. Our agents handle sensitive data responsibly and deliver consistent experiences across channels.

Workflow Integration

We integrate your custom agents into existing business processes, CRMs, collaboration platforms and data pipelines. This ensures AI adds value where it matters and remains manageable at scale.

Compliance & Governance

Our designs adhere to enterprise standards for security and governance. We incorporate audit trails, explainability and approval controls to help your organisation stay compliant with policies and regulations.

Business Outcomes

Improved Efficiency

Automate repetitive tasks and free up your teams for higher‑value work. Structured workflows and deterministic agents reduce time and errors.

Reduced Risk

Reliability and evaluation are built in from day one, minimising the risk of incorrect or unsafe behaviour. Agents operate within defined boundaries and integrate human oversight.

Better Decision‑Making

Your agents surface the right information to the right people. Workflow orchestration and Graph retrieval ensure responses are grounded and context‑rich.

Scalable Innovation

By following the crawl‑walk‑run approach, you can safely scale your AI initiatives across departments and use cases, from simple Q&A bots to complex multi‑agent systems.
